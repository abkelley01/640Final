Teacher Resources

Our teacher resources have been compiled by our staff team with peer reviews from professionals not directly involved in the creation of this website. Our topics cover history handouts to use within the classroom and teacher resource packets which have been compiled by various museums on topics pertaining to their historic site. If you would like to submit your own resource for review to be added to our list, please submit your resource here.

Carlyle House Historic Park


History in a Haversack (K-4):

The content in this packet contains several small articles, a few advertisements from the Carlyle businesses, and questions to help lead discussion on the time period and the various things that could be found within a haversack. (SOL: K.2, 1.1, 3.6, VS.1/US.1, VS.4)
